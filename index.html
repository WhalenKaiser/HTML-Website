Welcome to My New Website

This website is written using HTML5, Bootstrap, and CSS3. I'm hosting it on AWS in an S3 container. There's a page of this site devoted to ongoing programming projects and links to check out more.

I originally wanted to host a WordPress site on AWS, with full failover, load balancing, and security. That proved to be too financially intense due to hosting costs. So, I'll be experimenting with this format and later, probably with a Django site. I'm trying to find pricing for hosting a Django blog with AWS.

UK Living

Where am I from? Why am I here? Is that a goat?

I'm from the US. I grew up in Oklahoma, spent a few years in Texas, and lived a few more in Atlanta, Georgia to be specific. Atlanta was my favorite. I'm here because of my husband's work. His current situation lasts until the summer of 2021 and then we'll see what's next. And, yes, that's a goat.

Meetok

Meetok is a small plastic toy representing a kodoma from the anime movie, Princess Mononoke. My best friend's children sent him to me and we play a game where I take photos of Meetok around the UK and make up stories about him.

So, in this section I will integrate a blog feature or try out the instagram connection features. The possibilities will be fun to research.

Art

As a detail-oriented person with free time, I tend to have several art projects going on much of the time. So, I'll be posting those here and probably exploring ways to hook in nifty artist-directed website features.

I didn't move with my art supplies and our space is very different here, so my current art projects are mostly drawing, photography, and embroidery. Drawing is just the one thing all art people learn and embroidery is a skill my grandmother taught me before I could read. Yeah, it only occurred to me recently that I was taught handcrafts like a mid-evil kid. It was a tactic she used to make me stop running through the mud and hold still.
